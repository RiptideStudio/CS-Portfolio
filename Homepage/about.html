Hi, I'm Callen!

Programmer, Game Developer, Creator.

I'm a game developer and programmer currently studying in Redmond, Washington. I like to take elements from things I enjoy and bring them to life in my own work. Art, films, music, or anything that really captures my interest. You can probably tell by my project section that I watch a lot of movies!

I tend to make lots of custom game engines, but not because I'd use it over something like Godot or Unreal. It poses a significant design and technical problem. I was taught to be an engineer, which means opening up the notorious black box and figuring out how things works. It's also really great coding practice.
