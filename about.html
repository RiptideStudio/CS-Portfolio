Hi, I'm Callen!

Programmer, Game Developer, Creator.

I've been making games since I was 13 years old, regardless of whether or not they ended up on Steam (or in the hands of my little brother!)

One thing I've learned recently is that game development is multifaceted. To really make something good, you need to understand everything: how an artist thinks, how a programmer writes code, and how a musician brings a song to life.

This has become a core element of the kind of stories I want to tell.
Right now, I'm developing my next indie game title, Earthward. Go check it out!
